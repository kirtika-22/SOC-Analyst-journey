# Day 15 - Interview Questions with Answers (SIEM Tools)

## 1. What is a SIEM tool?

**Answer:**
A SIEM (Security Information and Event Management) tool collects logs from multiple devices, normalizes and correlates them, detects suspicious activities, generates alerts, and helps SOC analysts investigate security incidents.

---

## 2. Why do organizations use different SIEM tools?

**Answer:**
Organizations have different requirements based on their size, budget, infrastructure, cloud adoption, compliance needs, and existing technology stack. Therefore, they choose the SIEM that best fits their environment.

---

## 3. Name some popular SIEM tools.

**Answer:**
- Splunk
- IBM QRadar
- Microsoft Sentinel
- ArcSight
- Wazuh
- ELK Stack
- Graylog

---

## 4. Which SIEM tool is considered the market leader?

**Answer:**
Splunk is considered the market leader because of its powerful search capabilities, analytics, dashboards, and extensive integrations.

---

## 5. What is Splunk?

**Answer:**
Splunk is a commercial enterprise SIEM platform that provides log collection, searching, monitoring, analytics, visualization, and security incident detection.

---

## 6. What are the advantages of Splunk?

**Answer:**
- Powerful search (SPL)
- Excellent dashboards
- Advanced analytics
- Large integration ecosystem
- Widely used in enterprises

---

## 7. What is the biggest disadvantage of Splunk?

**Answer:**
It is expensive because licensing is based on the amount of data ingested daily.

---

## 8. What is IBM QRadar?

**Answer:**
IBM QRadar is an enterprise SIEM that provides strong threat detection, event correlation, compliance management, and threat intelligence integration.

---

## 9. What is Microsoft Sentinel?

**Answer:**
Microsoft Sentinel is Microsoft's cloud-native SIEM and SOAR solution that runs on Azure and provides AI-powered threat detection and automated incident response.

---

## 10. Why is Microsoft Sentinel called cloud-native?

**Answer:**
Because it is built specifically for cloud environments and runs on Microsoft Azure without requiring on-premise infrastructure.

---

## 11. What is ArcSight?

**Answer:**
ArcSight is one of the oldest enterprise SIEM platforms known for powerful event correlation and compliance reporting.

---

## 12. What is Wazuh?

**Answer:**
Wazuh is a free and open-source SIEM/XDR platform used for log monitoring, threat detection, file integrity monitoring, vulnerability detection, and endpoint security.

---

## 13. Why is Wazuh popular among students?

**Answer:**
Because it is free, lightweight, open source, and ideal for building SOC labs and gaining hands-on experience.

---

## 14. What is ELK Stack?

**Answer:**
ELK Stack is an open-source log management platform consisting of:
- Elasticsearch
- Logstash
- Kibana

It is mainly used for searching, analyzing, and visualizing logs.

---

## 15. What does Elasticsearch do?

**Answer:**
Elasticsearch stores, indexes, and searches log data quickly.

---

## 16. What does Logstash do?

**Answer:**
Logstash collects, processes, filters, and forwards logs from different sources.

---

## 17. What does Kibana do?

**Answer:**
Kibana provides dashboards, graphs, charts, and visualizations for log data.

---

## 18. What is Graylog?

**Answer:**
Graylog is an open-source log management platform focused on easy log collection, centralized logging, alerting, and visualization.

---

## 19. What can SIEM do?

**Answer:**
- Collect logs
- Normalize logs
- Correlate events
- Detect suspicious activity
- Generate alerts
- Create dashboards
- Produce compliance reports
- Assist in incident investigations

---

## 20. What can SIEM NOT do?

**Answer:**
- Prevent attacks by itself
- Replace EDR/XDR
- Automatically stop malware
- Detect every attack without tuning
- Replace SOC analysts

---

## 21. Which SIEM tools are free?

**Answer:**
- Wazuh
- ELK Stack
- Graylog

---

## 22. Which SIEM tool is best for beginners?

**Answer:**
Wazuh, because it is free, easy to deploy, and provides practical SOC experience.

---

## 23. Which SIEM is best for cloud environments?

**Answer:**
Microsoft Sentinel.

---

## 24. Which SIEM is highly customizable?

**Answer:**
ELK Stack.

---

## 25. Which SIEM focuses mainly on log management?

**Answer:**
Graylog.

---

## 26. What are the latest SIEM trends?

**Answer:**
- Cloud-native SIEM
- AI/ML-powered analytics
- Real-time threat detection
- SIEM + SOAR integration
- Increased automation
- Threat intelligence integration

---

# HR/Scenario-Based Questions

## 27. If your company uses a SIEM you have never worked on, what will you do?

**Answer:**
I understand SIEM fundamentals such as log collection, normalization, correlation, alerting, and dashboards. Since these concepts are similar across platforms, I can quickly learn a new SIEM using documentation and hands-on practice.

---

## 28. Which SIEM would you choose for learning as a fresher?

**Answer:**
I would choose Wazuh because it is free, open source, widely used for SOC labs, and provides practical experience.

---

## 29. Why should a SOC Analyst know multiple SIEM tools?

**Answer:**
Different organizations use different SIEM platforms. Knowing multiple tools improves adaptability and increases job opportunities.

---

## 30. Which SIEM would you recommend for a large enterprise?

**Answer:**
Splunk or IBM QRadar because they provide enterprise-grade security, advanced analytics, scalability, and strong support.
