# Day 02 Interview Questions

## Q1. What is a SOC?

Answer:
SOC stands for Security Operations Center. It is responsible for monitoring, detecting, analyzing and responding to cyber threats.

---

## Q2. What is Alert Triage?

Answer:
Alert triage is the process of analyzing alerts to determine whether they are true positives or false positives.

---

## Q3. What is Escalation?

Answer:
Escalation means forwarding confirmed security incidents to higher-level analysts (L2/L3).

---

## Q4. What is SIEM?

Answer:
SIEM stands for Security Information and Event Management. It collects and analyzes logs from multiple devices.

---

## Q5. Name three SIEM tools.

Answer:
- Splunk
- Wazuh
- ELK

---

## Q6. What is VirusTotal?

Answer:
VirusTotal is a threat intelligence platform used to analyze suspicious files, URLs and IP addresses.
