# Importance of Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity protects systems, networks, and data from cyber attacks. It ensures business continuity, protects sensitive information, and maintains customer trust.

---

# Security Operations Center (SOC)

A Security Operations Center (SOC) is a centralized team responsible for monitoring, detecting, investigating, and responding to security incidents 24/7.

### Functions of SOC
- Continuous Monitoring
- Threat Detection
- Incident Response
- Log Analysis
- Threat Intelligence
- Reporting and Documentation

---

# Threat, Vulnerability and Risk

### Threat
Anything that can cause damage to a system.

Example:
- Malware
- Hacker
- Phishing Attack

### Vulnerability
A weakness that can be exploited by a threat.

Example:
- Weak Password
- Unpatched Software

### Risk
The possibility that a threat will exploit a vulnerability and cause damage.

Risk = Threat × Vulnerability

---

# CIA Triad

### Confidentiality
Ensures information is accessible only to authorized users.

Methods:
- Encryption
- Access Control
- Multi-Factor Authentication

### Integrity
Ensures information remains accurate and unmodified.

Methods:
- Hashing
- Digital Signatures

### Availability
Ensures systems and services remain available when needed.

Methods:
- Backup
- Redundancy
- Disaster Recovery
