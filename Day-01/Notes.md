## Key Notes

### What is SOC?
A Security Operations Center (SOC) is a centralized unit where cybersecurity professionals monitor , detect , analyze and respond to security incedents in real time using a combination of technologies and processes 

### Why is SOC Important?
- Monitors security 24×7
- Detects cyber attacks
- Responds to incidents
- Protects organizational assets

### Functions of a SOC
- continuous monitoring 
- threat detection  
- incident response 
- reporting and documentation 
- improvement loop

### Responsibilities of a SOC Analyst
- Monitor security alerts
- Analyze logs
- Investigate suspicious activities
- Escalate confirmed incidents
- Document findings

## 💡 What I Learned Today

- Every incident starts with an alert.
