# Day 1 - Interview Questions

## Q1. What is a SOC?

**Answer:**
A Security Operations Center (SOC) is a centralized unit where cybersecurity professionals monitor , detect , analyze and respond to security incedents in real time using a combination of technologies and processes 

---

## Q2. What is the role of a SOC Analyst?

**Answer:**
A SOC Analyst monitors alerts, analyzes logs, investigates suspicious activities, and responds to security incidents.

---

## Q3. What is an Alert?

**Answer:**
An alert is a notification generated by a security tool when suspicious activity is detected.

---

## Q4. What is an Incident?

**Answer:**
An incident is a confirmed security event that requires investigation and response.
